Project: Analysis of an individual water level recorder for salt marsh monitoring

Authors: Grant McKown (james.mckown@unh.edu), Jennifer Gibson (jennifer.gibson@unh.edu)

Organization: Coastal Habitat Restoration Team, Jackson Estuarine Laboratory, University of New Hampshire

Output Stats README

- The 'Output Stats' folder is the destination of finalized tables for the descriptive statistics of tidal hydrology of creek, groundwater, and pool water level recorders and flooding for groundwater level recoders and constructed sparrow islands. 

- The files exported to the folder are CSV files.
